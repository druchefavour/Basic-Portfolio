About Me

I am Dr. Angus Ogubuike. I have worked in successful and productive mufti-disciplinary teams in reputed multinational companies. With almost 20 years experience in reservoir engineering and a wide area of Oil and Gas business, I am technically sound and have demonstrated management and team leadership skills in corporate environments. I have solid technical experience in supporting investments, acquisition and divestment, operations reservoir engineering (offshore and onshore), study reservoir engineering (covering different types of rocks: Clastics, Carbonates, salt, fractures etc), consultancy, business development, portfolio management, economic evaluation, oil and gas field development planning, reserves evaluation, reservoir simulation modeling, value assurance processes, enhanced and improved oil recovery, new ventures and in driving new technology. Has championed the evaluation of exploration and exploitation assets for acquisition and divestment and has working knowledge in the US onshore, offshore and unconventional assets supporting teams and companies in identifying and evaluating technical and economic merits of new investment opportunities.

I am able to work well on own initiative and can demonstrate high level of motivation required to meet the tightest of deadlines. Under significant pressure, I possess strong ability to perform effectively. I am a well-organized team player who has worked in major Oil and Gas projects in Africa, China, Netherlands, the UK North Sea, Russia, US unconventional (Permian and the Bakken) and onshore brown fields in Texas. I have also worked on projects in countries such as Azerbaijan, Kurdistan, France, Denmark and Norway. I have been involved in opportunity generation, technical new-area capture and evaluation of Oil and gas resources in North West Europe, the Middle East, the Gulf of Mexico, the unconventional play in the United States and Africa. I possess Doctorate in Business Administration from the Robert Kennedy College, Zurich and am married with three children
